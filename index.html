About IWAN TECH LABS

Our Mission for Rural Karnataka

Empowering rural students with practical cybersecurity skills, regardless of their educational background or English proficiency.

Who We Are

IWAN TECH LABS is a rural-focused cybersecurity skill development initiative based in Channagiri, Karnataka. We specialize in making complex cybersecurity concepts accessible to students from rural backgrounds.

Our Core Philosophy

"Skills over degrees, practical over theoretical, and confidence over certificates. We believe every rural student has the potential to become a cybersecurity professional."

Why IWAN TECH LABS Started

We noticed three major gaps in rural cybersecurity education:

Language Barrier

Most cybersecurity resources are in English, creating hurdles for Hindi/Kannada medium students.

Degree Myth

Rural students believe they need computer science degrees to enter cybersecurity.

Geographical Barrier

Quality cybersecurity training was only available in metro cities.

IWAN TECH LABS was founded to bridge these gaps and make cybersecurity career paths accessible to every rural student in Karnataka.

    We understand that most rural students start with zero knowledge of cybersecurity. Our approach:

    1. Foundation Building

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common Questions Answered

    Quick answers to questions asked by rural students and parents.

    1. Degree chahiye cybersecurity seekhne ke liye?

    Answer: Nahi, degree ki zarurat nahi hai. IWAN TECH LABS programs ke liye kisi bhi educational background ke students apply kar sakte hain - PUC, ITI, Diploma, ya koi bhi stream.

    2. English nahi aati to kya kar sakte hain?

    Answer: English ki tension mat lo. Hum pehle Hindi ya Kannada mein samjhate hain. Technical terms slowly introduce karte hain. 90% explanation regional language mein hota hai.

    3. Laptop compulsory hai?

    Answer: Basic computer ya smartphone se start kar sakte hain. Training ke baad aapko laptop lena padega practical practice ke liye. Hum guidance denge affordable options ke baare mein.

    4. Fees installment mein de sakte hain?

    Answer: Discussion ke baad installment options available hain. Paise ke wajah se kisi bhi student ki learning band nahi honi chahiye.

    5. Certificate ka kya value hai?

    Answer: Hamare certificates verifiable hain website pe. Certificate practical skills ka proof hai. Companies skills dekhti hain, sirf certificate nahi.

    6. Kitna time lagta hai seekhne mein?

    Answer: Depends on student ki speed pe. Fast learners 2-3 months mein basic skills seekh lete hain. Slow learners ke liye extra time dete hain. Hum repeat karte hain jab tak samajh na aaye.

    7. Job guarantee hai kya?

    Answer: Nahi, job guarantee nahi dete. Hum skills sikhaate hain, job guidance dete hain. Job student ki mehnat aur market conditions pe depend karta hai.

    8. Parents ko kaise samjhayenge?

    Answer: Hum parents se direct baat karte hain. Simple language mein cybersecurity career ke baare mein samjhaate hain. Aap parents ko humse baat karwa sakte hain.

    9. Online ya offline class?

    Answer: Local students ke liye offline guidance available hai. Door ke students ke liye online support via WhatsApp and calls. Hybrid model follow karte hain.

    10. Age limit hai kya?

    Answer: Minimum 18 years age chahiye. Maximum koi limit nahi hai. Working professionals bhi join kar sakte hain career change ke liye.
